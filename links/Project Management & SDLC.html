Project Management/SDLC

I have keen interest on how project management gets connect to SDLC ("Software Development Life Cycle") as I have experience both managing software project from opening to closing with the help of SDLC frameworks.


Waterfall Model a sequential model where each phase must be completed before the next one begins.

V-shaped Model emphasizes the testing phase and its relationship to the corresponding development stages.

Agile Model an iterative and incremental approach that focuses on flexibility, collaboration, and rapid delivery some common frameworks includes "Scrum, Kanban, Lean, and Extreme Programming (XP)".

Big Bang Model a less structured model where there is little to no planning involved, development starts with the available resources and efforts and the final product may or may not meet the initial requirements.
